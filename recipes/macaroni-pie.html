Macaroni Pie

Description

Macaroni pie is a staple food in Trinidad & Tobago. It is similar to the food referred to as
Baked Macaroni and Cheese in the US, however the macaroni is more compact and can be
cut into chunks as opposed to scooped with a spoon.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 boxes of elbow macaroni
  • 4 bags of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika

Steps

  1. Bring 24 ounces of water in 2 large saucepans to boil.
  2. Once boiling, carefully pour the macaroni in the water, and cook until al dente.
  3. Once the macaroni is cooked, strain all the water from the pots.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  5. Thinly spread the butter on the bottom and sides of the metal pan that will cook
    the maraconi. Make sure that the edges of the pan are at least 2 inches high.
  6. Sprinkle a thin layer of cheese over the bottom of the pan.
  7. Pour half the macaroni in the pan. Add a bag of cheese, and mix.
  8. Pour the other half of the macaroni in the pan, add another bad of cheese, and mix.
  9. Add the third bad of cheese, the eggs, the heavy cream, and the salt to the macaroni
    and mix.
  10. Use a pot spoon to flatten and smooth the macaroni until it's flat. Then use the last
    bag of cheese to create a top layer of cheese over the macaroni.
  11. Sprinkle the paprika across the top of the cheese.
  12. Put the macaroni pan in the oven, and cooked uncovered for about 30 minutes, or
    until the cheese crust is a golden brown.
  13. Take the pie out of the oven, turn the oven off, plate, and enjoy! :)
