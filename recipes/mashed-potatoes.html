Mashed Potatoes

Description

This recipe gives you creamy, savory mashed potatoes, topped with a green garnish.

Ingredients

  • 10 Large Idaho Potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 5 scallion stalks, thinly chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Bring 12 ounces of water in a large saucepan to boil
  2. While waiting for the water to boil, rinse the potatoes, then chop each potato into 4 pieces.
  3. Put the potatoes in the water, cover the pot, and let them boil for about 25-35 minutes, or until the potatoes are soft enough to mash.
  4. Take one cup of the potato water and put it to the side. Then drain the rest of the water.
  5. Peel the skin off of the potato pieces.
  6. Add the rest of the ingredients (besides the scallions) to the pot.
  7. Mix the pot either with a hand masher or a mixer. If the potatoes seem to dry, add the saved potato water bit by bit until desired consistency is reached.
  8. Plate the potatoes, and sprinkle the scallions on top.
  9. Add salt to taste and enjoy! :)
