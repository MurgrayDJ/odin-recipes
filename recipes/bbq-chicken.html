BBQ Chicken Recipe

Description

Barbecue chicken is a common and popular food in the US, probably because it's pretty easy to make! We'll show you how below.

Ingredients

  • 8 room temperature chicken drumsticks
  • 1 bottle of your fave BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of adobo
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • A handful of thinly chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  2. While waiting for the oven to preheat, dab your chicken dry with a paper towel.
  3. Season the chicken with the adobo, onion, and garlic powder.
  4. Put the chicken in a covered metal pan and add enough water to create a thin
    layer on the bottom.
  5. Put the chicken in the oven for 45 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  6. Pull the chicken out, and cover it in the barbecue sauce.
  7. Put it back in the oven and cook uncovered for 10 - 15 minutes, or until the
    barbecue sauce looks slightly charred.
  8. Take the chicken out of the oven and sprinkle the parsley on top.
  9. Turn the oven off, plate the chicken, and enjoy! :)
